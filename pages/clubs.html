Drama Club

About Our Club

The Drama Club provides a creative outlet for students interested in theater, acting, and stage production. We produce several plays throughout the year and welcome students of all experience levels.

Club Activities

  • Weekly acting workshops and improvisation sessions
  • Two major productions per semester
  • Guest workshops with theater professionals
  • Collaborations with local theaters

About Our Club

For students passionate about technology, programming, and innovation. We host hackathons, workshops, and tech talks to help members develop their skills and connect with industry professionals.

  • Annual 24-hour hackathon
