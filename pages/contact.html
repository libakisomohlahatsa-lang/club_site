Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new club?

To start a new club, you need at least 5 interested students, a faculty advisor, and a completed club registration form. Submit your proposal to the Clubs Hub office for review.

Can I join multiple clubs?

Absolutely! Students are encouraged to explore different interests by joining multiple clubs. There's no limit to how many clubs you can be part of.

Are there any membership fees?

Most clubs are free to join. Some specialized clubs may have minimal fees for materials or equipment, but these are always clearly communicated upfront.

How do I get funding for club events?

Clubs can apply for funding through the Student Government Association. Submit a detailed event proposal and budget at least 4 weeks before your event.

Can alumni join clubs?

While clubs are primarily for current students, some clubs welcome alumni participation. Contact the specific club for their alumni policy.

How do I reserve space for club meetings?

Room reservations can be made through the Clubs Hub office. Submit your request at least 72 hours in advance for regular meetings.
